I collected all comments made by users on the 500 most famous apps, and here are the results I found after filtering the stop words (most common words in the English language). First, there are many positive words such as 'great', 'fun' or 'love', which shows that users tend to express their satisfaction for these famous apps. This might have convinced people to download an app when they were hesitant to do it, which contributes to the success of that app. Another important category of comments is the suggestions that users make to the developers. Comments containing 'fix', 'update' or 'please' show that the interaction user-developer seems important, and maybe when the developer takes these suggestions into account, the fame of the app increases since users are more satisfied. Finally, it seems that Gaming apps receive a lot of comments since the word 'game' is the second most famous one after 'app'.
